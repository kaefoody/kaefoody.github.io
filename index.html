Which control panel do I have?

I authored this accessible end-user guide for an electronics manufacturing company because their clients couldn't identify or locate their product's model number or basic featuress.

By nature, their product (high-voltage electrical control panels) was simply too technical and intimidating for their end-users (average homeowners) to approach. Of course, their support staff needed these basic pieces of information before they could even begin to troubleshoot.

I authored this guide to safely help users find these details before they even picked up the phone. As a result, their end-users had a more productive and pleasant support experience, while support staff gained back valuable time spent on these calls.
