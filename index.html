How to Find the Model Number for your Boulay Fab Panel

I authored this accessible end-user guide during my time as a technical writer at Boulay Fab, because I noticed our staff was spending a lot of time on support calls.

When our end users called for support, they often couldn't identify their model number or their panel's basic features. These details were necessary before we could even begin our troubleshotting process.

As a solution, I compiled this guide to help our users identify their panel's model number and specs, so that their support experience was more productive and enjoyable. On our end, this significantly reduced the time we spent on support calls.
