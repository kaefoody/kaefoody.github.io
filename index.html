Find and understand your electrical control panel's ratings label

I authored this accessible end-user guide for an electronics manufacturing company because their clients couldn't identify their product's model number or basic features.

Their product (high-voltage electrical control panels) was too technical and intimidating for their end-users (average homeowners) to approach. However, their support staff needed to know these basic pieces of information before they could even begin to troubleshoot.

This guide helps users safely locate these details before they contact support. As a result, the company's end-users have a more efficient and pleasant support experience, and support staff have gained back valuable time spent on these calls.
